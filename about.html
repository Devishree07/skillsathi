About SkillSaathi

SkillSaathi is a platform built for Bharat — connecting skilled local workers to real job opportunities, even if they don’t have smartphones. With missed calls, WhatsApp access, and local support centers, we make sure no worker is left behind in the digital economy.

Our mission is to empower electricians, plumbers, tailors, masons, and many others by giving them visibility, income, and respect through simple technology.
